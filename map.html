The south-east pays most

Anambra, Imo, Edo and Rivers sit at the top on almost every metric, while the northern producing states — Borno, Katsina, Zamfara — sit at the bottom.

Arcs are gaps, not shipments

An arc shows that the same food costs several times more at one end than the other. It is a measure of market fragmentation, not a record of trade actually flowing.

Read the index, not the naira

The staple basket adds one unit each of eight foods priced in mixed units, so it is a comparative yardstick between states rather than a real weekly shop.
