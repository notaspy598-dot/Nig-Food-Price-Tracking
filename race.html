The axis is the story

Watch the scale at the bottom. It stretches from roughly ₦2,000 to well past ₦9,000 — the race is run on ground that keeps moving.

Fish start ahead and stay ahead

Dried and smoked fish lead from the first frame, but they climb slowest in percentage terms. The fastest climbers are grains and tubers starting far down the field.

Prices, not baskets

Each item is priced in its own unit — a bottle of oil, a crate of eggs, a kilo of rice — so the ranking compares items to themselves over time, not shopping-cart value.
